What's we do:

Chaser is an App to keep up with rare, limited and seasonal beers. We help you track release schedules for your favorite breweries in one place

What's our mission?

To make finding and drinking amazing beers easier than ever. We've all had that one beer that keeps slipping through our fingers. That delicious winter warmer or barrel-aged stout that we just keep missing the release of. Well chaser helps make keeping track of all those crazy release schedules or knowing which variants are available this month that much easier.
